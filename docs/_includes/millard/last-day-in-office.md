Washington, March 3. 1853.

Charles Van Benthuysen Esq.

My Dear Sir,

I beg leave to return you my thanks for a copy of that indispensible \[sic\] "<u>Manual</u>" to every New Yorker, "The Red Book."

I observe that its typography does credit to the publisher.

I am Respectfully

Yours
