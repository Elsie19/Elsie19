*To the Senators of the United States, respectively.*

SIR: Whereas divers and weighty causes connected with executive business necessary to be transacted create an extraordinary occasion requiring that the Senate be convened, you are therefore requested, as a member of that body, to [attend a meeting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/31st_United_States_Congress) thereof to be holden at the Capitol, in the city of Washington, on the 4th day of March instant.
