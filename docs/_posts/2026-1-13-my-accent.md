# Innovations

## Intrusive /l/

I have a sound rule where:

> oʊ > o̞ː /\_\_ɫ/

Thus:

* ⟨lonely⟩ /ˈɫoʊn.li/ -> ⟨lolnely⟩ /ˈɫo̞ːɫn.li/
* ⟨both⟩ /ˈboʊθ/ -> ⟨bolth⟩ /ˈbo̞ːɫθ/
* ⟨only⟩ /ˈoʊn.li/ -> ⟨olnly⟩ /ˈo̞ːɫn.li/
* ⟨old⟩ /ˈoʊld/ -> ⟨old⟩ /ˈo̞ːɫd/

## Owl tensing

> aʊ(.ə) > æ /\_\_l/

Thus:

* ⟨owl⟩ /aʊ(.ə)l/ -> ⟨æl⟩ /æ(.ə)l/
* ⟨vowel⟩ /ˈvaʊ.əl/ -> ⟨væl⟩ /ˈvæ(.ə)l/
